Welcome to

Lango!

Customize your vocabulary

Lango is a flashcard app that helps people learn Chinese. Users can log in with their gmail accounts, store the flashcards that they created, and start reviewing.

Instructions

  1. Log in with your gmail account.
  2. Enter the English word in the left box.
  3. Press Enter to translate it into Chinese.
  4. Click "Save" if you want to save the flashcard.
  5. If you are ready to review, click "Start Review".
  6. Type in the correct English translation.
  7. Either press Enter or click on the flashcard to check answer.
  8. You can go back and forth between the Add and Review page anytime you want.
  9. Enjoy learning!
